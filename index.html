Common Questions & Concerns

We understand this is a stressful time. Here are answers to help you make the right decision.

"How much will this cost? Will my insurance cover it?"

We work directly with your insurance company to maximize your coverage. Most water damage is covered by homeowner's insurance. We'll handle all documentation and communication with your adjuster. No upfront costs — we bill insurance directly. Free inspection and estimates always.

"How fast can you really get here?"

Our teams are strategically stationed across Houston for true 30-minute response times. We have crews in Sugar Land, The Woodlands, Katy, and central Houston. When you call, we dispatch the nearest available team immediately — 24 hours a day, 365 days a year, including holidays.

"Will I have to move out during restoration?"

Most customers can stay in their homes during restoration. We use containment barriers to isolate work areas and HEPA air scrubbers to maintain air quality. If temporary relocation is needed (rare), we'll help coordinate with your insurance for additional living expenses (ALE) coverage.

"How do I know you're legitimate and qualified?"

We're IICRC certified (Institute of Inspection Cleaning and Restoration Certification), fully licensed (License #VET-2023-1847), and carry $2M in liability insurance. BBB accredited with an A+ rating. Over 15 years serving Houston with 160+ five-star Google reviews. We'll provide all credentials upfront.

"What if mold has already started growing?"

We're certified mold remediation specialists. Mold can begin within 24-48 hours, which is why our rapid response is critical. We use antimicrobial treatments, HEPA vacuums, and negative air pressure to safely remove mold. All work follows EPA and IICRC S520 standards. Mold testing and clearance certificates provided.

"Can you handle the rebuild too, or will I need another contractor?"

We're your single source from emergency to move-in ready. Unlike others who only extract water, we handle complete reconstruction — drywall, flooring, painting, even kitchen and bathroom rebuilds. One team, one point of contact, one warranty. No coordination headaches between multiple contractors.

"What if my insurance company gives me trouble?"

We have 15+ years experience dealing with insurance adjusters. We document everything with photos, moisture readings, and detailed reports. Our team knows exactly what insurers need to approve claims quickly. If there's pushback, we'll advocate for you with supplemental claims and proper documentation. We fight to get you maximum coverage.

"How long will the whole restoration process take?"

Water extraction: 1-2 days. Drying and dehumidification: 3-5 days. Reconstruction varies based on damage extent — minor repairs take 1-2 weeks, major rebuilds 4-6 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline after inspection and keep you updated daily. We work efficiently without cutting corners.
